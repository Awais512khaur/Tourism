Tour Hub is a travel platform providing a free listing of travel products for travel agents in Pakistan.

Across the website, we collect user information in different ways and this document explains on how we collect and use that data and the associated security and privacy parameters.

Collection of Data

On the website, we collect users’ personal information such as name and their booking schedules. All this information is stored on our dedicated servers.

How We Save Your Data?

Your passwords and sensitive information are saved in our database in an encrypted format.

How We Use Your Data?

We don’t share your data with any marketing companies. However, we have verified travel partners who might get access to certain data elements.

Use of Cookies

We do save cookies on your computer/device when you login to improve your website experience.


Have More Questions? Contact Us

You can always contact us for any general queries or questions about your privacy of data.
